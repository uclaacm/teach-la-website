Robotics Fun with BEAM

BEAM at UCLA X ACM Teach LA

We partnered with BEAM at UCLA to teach 4th and 5th graders some cool robotics! We have 5 weeks of hour long lessons and you can follow along what we did here!

Setup

In these lessons, we will learn how to code mBots! Before these lessons, you may want to acquire an mBot which you will learn to program, and download mBlock which will be where you code your robot.

Week 1: Intro to Coding

How do people make games and websites and all that cool stuff? Learn the basics of coding with mBlock and block code! By the end of this lesson, you will learn how to make this spicy game .

Week 2: Robot Coding Basics

Explore more with mBlock and learn how to make a bug move just like how a robot would. By the end of this lesson we will have completed our second game!

Student Guide Instructor Guide

Week 3: Assembling our Robot

Beep boop beep boop. Learn how to put together your robot! Once we’ve assembled our robot, ASME at UCLA will come in and teach you a bit about the fancy robots and Battle Bots we make in university.

Week 4: Coding our Robot

Robots. Coding. Finally time to code robots?? In this lesson, you will learn how to make your robot move at your command!

Week 5: Making our Robot Dance

In this final lesson, we will learn how to make our robot dance! Want to learn how to make your robot do more cool things like how to follow a line? See below for more robot ideas and materials!
