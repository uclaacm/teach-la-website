Accountability and Transparency

At ACM Teach LA, we're committed to our mission of providing equitable access to computer science education. Part of that promise includes a commitment to accountability and transparency.

On this page, you'll find publicly-available budget statements starting from our 2019 academic year. We've included a set of graphics that help you navigate where we get our money from and how we spend it; you are also free to view our data in tabular form, or download a spreadsheet or CSV of our budget statement.
