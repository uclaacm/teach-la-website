Join Us!

Interested in learning how you can help make computer science education more accessible? Then you've come to the right place!

Bear in mind that all of our positions are zero experience required, and all majors are welcome! Teach LA is about growth at all levels; not just in the kids we teach, but in our team. Want to be a teacher? We'll teach you the material and how to teach it to others! Want to be a developer? We'll train you with all the technologies we use.

The easiest and most direct way to join is by attending our upcoming fall general meeting: 7:30pm PDT, October 6th, 2020! To receive updates before then, check out our Facebook and fill out our Interest Form.

Want to learn more? Check out role responsibilities below to see exactly what our organization has in store.

Teaching

Teachers are the lifeblood of Teach LA. They provide kids in the LAUSD with high quality CS education in-class. Interested? You can find out more by attending our GBM 7:30pm PDT, October 6th, 2020! Here's a quick overview of what being an instructor entails:

  • Directly interface with teachers and students
  • Play a key role in curriculum development by providing direct feedback from teaching
  • Learn what you teach, teach what you learn

Curriculum

Our curriculum developers are the lifeblood of Teach LA. They create all the material that we teach, and ensure that any feedback from our schools is met with swift implementation. Interested? Here's some more of the responsibilities:

  • Work with school leads to build our curriculum
  • Work to constantly update and evolve Teach LA's pedagogy
  • Develop new courses
  • In addition to instructor meetings, attend curriculum meetings

Dev

Teach LA has a vibrant developer team culture that we'd love to have you join! Here's what we do:

  • Maintain our larger projects: the Teach LA Editor and its backend
  • Create interactive learning modules to present complicated topics to kids
  • Propose, develop, and deliver software to help Teach LA's mission!
  • Trainings and mentorship from lead developers in a variety of technologies!
  • Flexible committment - as much as you want, when you want

Board

Interested in not only helping kids and our mission, but helping our organization? Consider joining the Teach LA board! We have a variety of positions for you to investigate, which are discussed in greater detail as elections approach in winter quarter:

  • President
  • Curriculum Director
  • School Lead
  • Outreach Director
  • Logistics Director
  • Special Events Director
  • Advisor
  • Dev Team Director
